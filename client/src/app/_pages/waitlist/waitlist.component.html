Waitinglist Application

This application was developed to streamline a referral for cardiac surgery. All the information about the patient is presented to the surgeon. This information includes the angiogram, echo's and other additional investigations that are relevant to the referral. With all the information about a potential surgical case in one place, the surgeon can quickly estimate the surgical risk and envisioned benefit of the surgical procedure. The main benefit of this application is the significant reduction of administrative tasks that accompany a referral for cardiac surgery. There are two sides of this application, at one side we find the physician who refers a patient to the hospital that is going to operate on the patient. On the other side we can see the heartteam in the receiving hospital that is going to establish the indication for surgery and plan the date of surgery for this patient
